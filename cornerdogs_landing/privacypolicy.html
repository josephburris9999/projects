Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how all9s Solutions LLC handles information related to the Corner Dogs website. Corner Dogs is a portfolio demonstration application and is not a real restaurant ordering system.

Information You Provide

This landing website does not include account registration, payment forms, or a contact form. If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address, message content, and any information you choose to include. The linked demo app may ask for a name and hot dog order details to demonstrate a multi-step form flow.

Demo Order Information

The Corner Dogs demo is designed for demonstration only. It does not intentionally save submitted names or order details to a database, charge payment information, email an order, or send an order to a real restaurant.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, hosting providers, browsers, and security tools may process basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, pages requested, referring pages, and request timestamps. This information is used to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website.

Analytics

The site uses Google Analytics to understand general site usage, such as page views, browser information, approximate location, and referring pages. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to provide those measurements.

Third-Party Services

The site uses Google Analytics and loads Bootstrap and Bootstrap Icons from jsDelivr CDN. Your browser may request those assets and services directly from those providers.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the website, respond to messages, protect the website, maintain business records, and improve the visitor experience.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers who help operate the website, when required by law, or when necessary to protect our rights, users, or services.

Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate for a small informational website. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may contact us to ask questions about information you have provided, request an update, or ask that we delete information when we are not required to retain it.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date.

Contact

Questions regarding Corner Dogs may be directed to all9s Solutions LLC through hello@all9ssolutions.com. Source code, issue reporting, and project releases are available through the project's GitHub repositories.
